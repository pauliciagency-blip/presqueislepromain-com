Who We Are

Presque Isle Professional Maintenance, LLC

We are a family owned and operated commercial cleaning company proudly serving Erie, PA and the surrounding communities. We believe that a clean workplace reflects your professionalism — and we take that responsibility seriously every single time we walk through your door.

We provide our cleaning professionals with ongoing training that includes safety and regulations, how to properly clean, how to use our products and equipment, as well as how to always maintain outstanding customer service to all businesses.

We strive to provide excellent service in every job we do and match this with our excellent customer service — there for you, every step of the way.
